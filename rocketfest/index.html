RocketFest International

RocketFest International is a tournament organized by RuyDiazMiocid in partnership with RCorp, bringing together many players of many different nationalities on the game Rocket League and broadcast live on Ruydiazmiocid's Twitch channel.

The last tournament was held on December 30, 2020, but it's coming back soon for your enjoyment.

The technical part is managed by RCorp. If you have any suggestions or discover a bug, please let us know via the contact form.
